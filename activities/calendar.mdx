## Overview

The Calendar in Nexus Search provides a centralized view of all your scheduled activities, meetings, and important dates. It integrates seamlessly with tasks, meetings, and other activities.

## Key Features

- **Unified View**: See all your scheduled activities in one place
- **Integration**: Automatically syncs with meetings, tasks, and deadlines
- **Multiple Views**: Switch between day, week, month, and agenda views
- **Team Calendar**: View team schedules and coordinate activities

## Use Cases

The Calendar is essential for:
- Scheduling and managing recruitment activities
- Coordinating interviews and meetings
- Tracking deadlines and important dates
- Planning team resources and availability
