## Overview

Meetings in Nexus Search help you coordinate interviews, client meetings, and team discussions. They integrate with your calendar and provide a structured way to manage appointments.

## Key Features

- **Meeting Scheduling**: Create and schedule meetings with participants
- **Interview Management**: Organize candidate interviews and assessments
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync with your calendar system
- **Attendee Management**: Track participants and send invitations

## Use Cases

Meetings are used for:
- Conducting candidate interviews
- Client presentations and discussions
- Team coordination meetings
- Stakeholder briefings
