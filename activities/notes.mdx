## Overview

Notes provide a flexible way to capture and organize information in Nexus Search. They can be attached to any entity and help you document important details and insights.

## Key Features

- **Quick Capture**: Easily jot down notes during calls, meetings, or research
- **Rich Formatting**: Use formatting options to structure your notes
- **Attachments**: Link notes to candidates, jobs, or other entities
- **Search**: Find notes quickly with full-text search

## Use Cases

Notes are perfect for:
- Documenting candidate screening insights
- Recording meeting outcomes and action items
- Capturing client requirements and feedback
- Maintaining research and market intelligence
