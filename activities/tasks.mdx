## Overview

Tasks help you organize and track your to-do items in Nexus Search. They can be associated with candidates, jobs, or other entities to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

## Key Features

- **Task Management**: Create, assign, and track tasks
- **Priorities**: Set task priorities and due dates
- **Assignments**: Assign tasks to team members
- **Related Entities**: Link tasks to candidates, jobs, or projects

## Use Cases

Tasks are used for:
- Following up with candidates
- Coordinating recruitment activities
- Tracking action items from meetings
- Managing daily workflows
