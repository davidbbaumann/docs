## Overview

The Email feature in Nexus Search allows you to send, receive, and track email communications with candidates, clients, and team members directly from the platform.

## Key Features

- **Email Integration**: Connect your email account for seamless communication
- **Email Templates**: Use pre-built templates for common scenarios
- **Tracking**: Monitor email opens, clicks, and responses
- **Thread Management**: Keep email conversations organized by context

## Use Cases

Emails are essential for:
- Communicating with candidates throughout the recruitment process
- Sending job offers and rejection letters
- Client updates and reporting
- Automated campaign communications
