<Update label="CW 10 | March 2026">
  ## New Features

  ### Outlook Calendar Sync

  - Your personal Outlook Calendar is now synchronized. This displays Outlook appointments in Nexus for the user who owns the calendar. It allows the Nexus AI to be aware of all relevant calendar events and use this as context for scheduling or other queries.
  - The Nexus AI Notetaker (Meeting Bot) can now join Teams meetings directly.
  - Meeting recordings and transcripts are linked directly in Nexus to the corresponding Microsoft meeting.

  ### Bulk Actions for Records

  Bulk actions are now available in list views for [Contacts](/core-concepts/contacts), [Candidates](/core-concepts/candidates), [Applications](/core-concepts/applications), and [Accounts](/core-concepts/accounts). When multiple records are selected by checking the checkbox on the left side of the table, a menu appears at the top of the table. The following actions are currently available:

  **Bulk Email**

  Send emails to the primary email address of all selected records. Use email templates and variables to personalize the message.

  **Bulk Delete**

  Delete multiple records such as Accounts, Contacts, Projects, Candidates, or Applications. This permanently removes the record along with any associated documents. Activities are deleted if there are no remaining references to other records.

  **Bulk Export to Excel (.xlsx) and CSV (.csv)**

  Select multiple records and choose the fields you want to export, as well as the file format — either Excel (.xlsx) or CSV (.csv). The export is provided as a download.

  ### Nexus Assistant Now Also Provides Customer Support

  The Nexus Assistant can now access the Nexus documentation. This enables the assistant to answer questions about using and administering the application, for example:

  - How do I create tasks?
  - What is the difference between contacts and candidates?
  - How do I configure application forms?

  ## Improvements

  - AI summaries now supplement activity descriptions based on meeting and audio recordings. Existing descriptions are not deleted or overwritten — additions are appended and marked as `✨ AI Recording Summary`.
  - In the main calendar view, the **"Start Recording Bot"** button is now displayed when the Nexus AI Notetaker is able to join an online meeting.

  ## Bug Fixes

  - Fixed errors that occurred when uploading files.
  - Deleting records such as accounts, contacts, etc. now also removes all associated files.
</Update>

<Update label="CW 09 | February 2026">
  ## New Features

  ### Content Management Agent (CMA) for images & creatives

  The Content Management Agent (CMA) analyzes images uploaded to Content Studio. When new images/creatives are uploaded, users can activate the Content Management Agent (activated by default). The CMA performs three main tasks:

  - **Description**: The uploaded image is provided with an (internal) description in natural language.
  - **Alt text**: Alternative texts relevant for public images are created. These are short, descriptive image descriptions that are also relevant for SEO.
  - **ARIA description**: Short descriptions are created explicitly in accordance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2, which are designed to increase accessibility on the web.

  ### Prompt Templates

  The new Prompt Template template type allows you to define recurring prompts for the AI, e.g., recurring analyses according to specific specifications, merging of information, or other tasks for the AI that are to be repeated in the same way, e.g., for different Accounts, Contacts, Projects, Candidates, or applications.

  ## Improvements

  ### Content Studio

  Templates can now be deleted and cloned/duplicated directly in the table overview.

  ## Bug Fixes

  - Duplicate success messages when cloning/deleting forms have been standardized.
  - Upload errors due to image uploads that are too large in Content Studio have been fixed. Only uploads up to a maximum of 15 MB per image are possible.
  - Various translation errors in the job section have been fixed.
  - Problems with white areas when scrolling on long pages have been resolved.
</Update>

<Update label="CW 08 | February 2026">
  ## New features

  ### Company logos from favicons (Account, Workspace, Application Forms)

  - To take advantage of the recognition value of company logos, the favicons of the company websites (always the first domain) are loaded and can now be seen in the list of companies and on their detail page.
  - The favicon of the domain specified in your workspace is used as the workspace logo.
  - Application forms use the favicon stored in your workspace and no longer the Nexus favicon to increase the branding effect.

  ### Content Studio

  Content Studio allows you to create ready-made and reusable templates. These can be used, for example, to quickly create emails, notes, tasks, or calls.

  All templates access variables and can thus be dynamically filled with content at the time of creation. The following templates are available:

  - Notes
  - Tasks
  - Meetings
  - Emails

  ## Improvements

  ### Activity Timeline

  - The buttons in the activity timeline now adjust to the screen width.
  - Sent and received email activities can no longer be edited/changed. Only the addition/removal of related records is permitted.
</Update>
