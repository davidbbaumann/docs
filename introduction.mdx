## Key Features

### Intelligent Relationship Management

Nexus Search centralizes all your recruitment relationships in one platform:

- **Accounts** - Manage client organizations and business relationships
- **Contacts** - Track decision-makers and hiring managers
- **Candidates** - Build and nurture your talent pool
- **Applications** - Monitor candidate submissions and progress

### Comprehensive Activity Tracking

Never lose track of your communications:

- **Activity Timeline** - Chronological view of all interactions
- **Calls** - Log phone conversations with detailed notes
- **Meetings** - Schedule and document in-person discussions
- **Emails** - Centralized email communication history
- **Notes** - Capture insights with voice input support

### AI-Powered Automation

Work smarter with intelligent features:

- **AI Profile Generation** - Automatically research and create company profiles
- **Data Management Agent (DMAA)** - Intelligent suggestions for updating records based on activity notes
- **Speech-to-Text** - Dictate notes and observations hands-free
- **Smart Extraction** - Automatically identify industry, company size, and decision-makers
