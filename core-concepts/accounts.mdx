## Overview

Accounts represent organizations or companies within Nexus Search. They serve as the top-level entity for managing business relationships.

## Key Features

- **Company Information**: Store detailed company profiles including name, industry, and contact details
- **Relationship Management**: Track interactions and relationships with organizations
- **Hierarchy**: Organize accounts in hierarchical structures for better management

## Use Cases

Accounts are essential for:
- Managing client organizations
- Tracking business relationships
- Organizing recruitment efforts by company
