## Overview

Candidates are individuals seeking employment opportunities. They form the talent pool and can be associated with multiple applications and jobs.

## Key Features

- **Talent Profiles**: Comprehensive candidate information including skills, experience, and qualifications
- **Application History**: Track all applications and interactions
- **Pipeline Management**: Monitor candidate status across different opportunities

## Use Cases

Candidates are central to:
- Building and maintaining talent pools
- Managing the recruitment process
- Tracking candidate relationships over time
- Creating talent pipelines for future opportunities
