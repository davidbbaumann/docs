## Overview

Campaigns are targeted outreach initiatives designed to attract and engage potential candidates. They enable structured communication and candidate sourcing.

## Key Features

- **Targeted Outreach**: Create focused recruitment campaigns for specific talent pools
- **Multi-Channel Communication**: Reach candidates through various channels
- **Performance Tracking**: Measure campaign effectiveness and ROI

## Use Cases

Campaigns are perfect for:
- Sourcing passive candidates
- Building talent pipelines
- Promoting employer brand
- Running targeted recruitment initiatives
